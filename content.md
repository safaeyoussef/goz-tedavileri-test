# Content Structure & Information Architecture

## 1. Home Page Sections
- **Hero:** Modern imagery of expert consultation. CTA: "Göz Sağlığınızı Keşfedin."
- **Goals:** Informative mission statement—educating Izmir and the world on eye health.
- **Treatments Grid:** 21 treatment cards with icons.
- **Latest Updates:** Magazine-style preview of recent eye-tech news.

## 2. Treatment Pages (21 Unique URLs)
*Target Treatments include: SMILE, LASIK, Akıllı Mercek (Smart Lenses), Vitrektomi, vb.*
- **Each page must include:**
  1. **Description:** High-level overview of the procedure.
  2. **Quick Info:** Duration, recovery time, and success rates.
  3. **FAQ:** 3 Schema-marked questions (e.g., "Acıtır mı?", "İyileşme süreci ne kadardır?").
  4. **Form:** Full Name, Country Code (Flag selection), Email, Message.

## 3. Innovative Features
- **Test Your Sight:** Interactive UI component. Users can simulate "Glaucoma" or "Cataract" vision through an image filter slider.
- **Magazine Format:** The "Güncel Haberler" section should use a grid layout with large featured images and "Read Time" indicators.
